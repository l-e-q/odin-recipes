Lasagna

This classic Lasagna Recipe is cheesy, saucy and full of flavor!

Lasagna is the best comfort food, made with noodles, ground beef, sauce and cheese. This lasagna is a hearty main dish that you can make ahead or bake right away.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt, Pepper and Italian Seasoning
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Egg
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Brown the ground beef with onion and garlic.
  3. To the pan, add the marinara sauce, Italian seasoning and fresh parsley. Simmer the sauce for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, stir together the ricotta cheese and an egg. Shred the mozzarella cheese.
  5. Layer the lasagna.
  6. Bake until hot and bubbly.
